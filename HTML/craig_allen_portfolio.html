Hi, my name’s Craig Allen and welcome to my portfolio. I am currently studying Interaction Design at Belfast School of Art. As well as design I also critique films, attempt to exercise and have a serious addiction to Game of Thrones.


I am a front end and UX designer who is never afraid of a challenge. Always keen to discover new ideas, learning is lifelong. I believe that everything serves a purpose, both in life and design. Most people say no, I say why not?
